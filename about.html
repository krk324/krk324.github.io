Thanks for visiting!

 

I'm Kaga, a Podcaster / Executive Coach / AI Advisor.

 

Feeling the limits of capitalist happiness, I explore "human mental wellness" (consciousness transformation and enlightenment experiences) from the perspectives of technology, psychology, neuroscience, Japanese culture, religion, narrative, philosophy, and entertainment.

 

After starting my career at Nomura Research Institute, I joined the CEO's office at BEENOS, focusing on venture investment and launching cross-border e-commerce businesses.
I founded startups in bento delivery and dating apps after graduating from General Assembly. In 2016, I joined Coincheck as the 2nd employee, leading overseas business development, marketing, HR, and Web development. During the ~$500M hacking incident, I coordinated with international organizations to lead tracking efforts.

 

Post-M&A, I joined Tradecraft in Silicon Valley, then ExaWizards in 2020 to launch a Well-being AI product, experiencing the company's IPO. In 2024, I also explored consciousness transformation through humor as a comedian "Kagayaki-man" at Titan Comedy School.

 

Currently, I host multiple podcasts at NEXUS Podcast Studio and operate STORYS.JP (since 2024). I also co-manage 11 beauty salons (ao.) with my wife.

 

A meditation practitioner who has undergone 12-day Vipassana meditation training and experienced a near-death experience through legal psilocybin session.

 

Hobbies: Reading, Karaoke, Angel Investing.
Born and raised in the Chicago, with roots in Japan Toyama. Father of two.
