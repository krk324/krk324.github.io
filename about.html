I am an entrepreneur/product designer with 5+ years experience in tech/crypto/blockchain startup scene. I was the 2nd hire of Coincheck, top 2 multi-billion cryptocurrency exchange in Japan. I'm now an advisor to them.

I helped grew a few-million dollars startup with 4 employees to the multi-billion company with 300+ employees by performing business development, growth marketing, programming, keynote speaking and hiring. I also networked with Fintech, Blockchain, and Cryptocurrency startups around the world for potential investments as Coincheck investment program lead.

I'm excited to take on a hybrid role at a crypto/blockchain startup where my duties include Product Design/Business Design/Biz Dev/Growth in Asian markets. I also invest in seed-stage startups.
Prior to joining Coincheck, I worked as an investment associate at BEENOS, a venture capital firm based in Tokyo, Japan. Then he founded HACKMAI, on demand bento delivery service in the States and online dating startup in Tokyo.
