Kagayaki "Kaga" Kawabata is product designer with experience founding two startups. He was a 2nd employee of unicorn cryptocurrency exchange Coincheck experiencing a buyout and now an advisor to Coincheck.

At Coincheck, he worked as Global Alliance Lead, overseeing global business development. He was also in charge of Coincheck investment program networking with Fintech, Blockchain, and Cryptocurrency startups around the world for potential investments. As a 2nd employee, he helped grow the company from the ground up by performing various roles in Coincheck such as business development, web development, hiring, UI/UX design, keynote speaking, and marketing.
Prior to joining Coincheck, he worked as an investment associate at BEENOS, a venture capital firm based in Tokyo, Japan. Then he founded HACKMAI on demand bento delivery service in the States and online dating startup in Tokyo.

"Taking risks is the best way to maximize the value of your life." - Kaga Kawabata
